Food Safety

Documented food safety with independent verification.

Virginia Produce Company complies with all food safety regulations and follows a GFSI-approved protocol through SQF standards.

  • Third-party food safety audits by SQF and customer review teams.
  • Comprehensive SOPs and SSOPs for sanitation and handling.
  • Independent lab testing and swabbing for microbiological verification.
  • Allergen controls and complete traceability through operations.
